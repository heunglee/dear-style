# Future Capabilities

## Impression Engine

Estimate perceived impressions from color and shape combinations.

Supported impressions:

- natural
- friendly
- elegant
- polished
- sophisticated
- professional
- soft
- bold

The engine must not evaluate beauty or attractiveness.

## Event Styling

Generate recommendations for contexts:

- interview
- wedding guest
- first date
- business meeting
- casual daily look
- evening dinner

## Virtual Try-On

Future rendering capabilities:

- lip products
- eyeliner styles
- eyebrow shapes
- eyewear
- clothing color overlays
- hair color overlays

## Personal Wardrobe

Allow users to store owned products and clothing.

Examples:

- lipsticks
- foundations
- eyeliners
- glasses
- tops
- accessories

## Beauty Copilot

Conversational assistant examples:

- What lip color should I wear with this navy dress?
- Which eyeliner style makes me look more approachable?
- Which colors should I avoid for an interview?

## Brand Intelligence

Aggregate anonymized patterns for Dear Daughter Beauty product development.

Possible outputs:

- common shade gaps
- cluster-specific color demands
- social preference vs self preference differences
- sensitive-skin user preferences
