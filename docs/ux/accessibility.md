# Accessibility

## Principles

Dear Style should be usable without relying only on color, fine motor precision, or perfect vision.

## Requirements

- Provide text alternatives for coaching overlays.
- Keep image quality warnings readable by screen readers.
- Use high contrast for overlay lines and points.
- Do not communicate state through color alone.
- Make all primary actions keyboard accessible on web.
- Provide clear labels for upload, capture, delete, and consent controls.
- Allow users to review instructions before opening camera flows.

## Motion and Camera

- Avoid unnecessary animation in coaching results.
- Do not auto-open camera without user action.
- Clearly explain why camera or photo access is requested.

## Feedback Tone

Accessibility includes emotional accessibility. Feedback should be specific, practical, and non-judgmental.
